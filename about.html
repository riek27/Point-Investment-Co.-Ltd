About Gold Medal Construction

Designing and building quality residential and commercial projects across South Sudan with excellence and precision.

Turning Dreams Into Reality

Gold Medal Construction (GMC) is a trusted construction and real estate company based in Juba, South Sudan. With over 10 years of experience in the industry, we specialize in designing and constructing residential homes, commercial buildings, petrol stations, and modern developments built to last.

Our goal is simple — to transform your vision into strong, beautiful, and functional developments. From planning and design to construction and finishing, we combine engineering expertise, skilled workmanship, and a strong commitment to quality.

We take pride in delivering reliable, affordable, and professional construction solutions that meet both local and international standards. Every project is handled with integrity, precision, and respect for our clients’ expectations.
